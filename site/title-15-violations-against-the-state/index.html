    Title 15 - Violations Against the State

    15(01). Treason

    No person shall

    a) aid‌ ‌in‌ ‌levying‌ ‌war‌ ‌against‌ ‌the‌ ‌state,‌ ‌adhering‌ ‌to‌ ‌its‌ ‌enemies,‌ ‌or‌ ‌giving‌ ‌them‌ ‌aid‌ ‌and‌ ‌comfort.‌ ‌Upon‌ ‌a‌ ‌trial‌ ‌for‌ ‌treason,‌ ‌the‌ ‌defendant‌ ‌cannot‌ ‌be‌ ‌convicted‌ ‌unless‌ ‌upon‌ ‌the‌ ‌testimony‌ ‌of‌ ‌two‌ ‌witnesses‌ ‌to‌ ‌the‌ ‌same‌ ‌overt‌ ‌act,‌ ‌or‌ ‌upon‌ ‌confession

    A violation of 15(01) is a Felony

    A violation of 15(01) carries a penalty of being taken by the FBI into a deep dark hole where you'll never be seen again

    15(02). Racketeering

    No person shall

    a) be affiliated with or be associated to‌ ‌a‌ ‌criminal‌ ‌organization,‌ ‌as‌ ‌described ‌by‌ ‌local‌ ‌or‌ ‌national‌ ‌law‌ ‌enforcement‌ ‌entities,‌ ‌and benefit or gain in any way from the organizations illicit acts

    A violation of 15(02) is a Felony

    A violation of 15(02) carries a penalty of 12 months of incarceration

    15(03). Money Laundering Instruments

    No person shall

    a) possess‌,‌ ‌hide,‌ ‌transfer,‌ ‌receive,‌ ‌or‌ ‌maintain ‌the‌ ‌storage‌ ‌of‌ ‌funds‌ ‌earned‌ ‌through‌ ‌comprehensive‌ ‌criminal‌ ‌activities

    b) intend ‌to‌ ‌transfer,‌ ‌hide,‌ ‌cycle,‌ ‌or‌ ‌deceive‌ the State in regards ‌funds‌ ‌collected‌ ‌through‌ ‌comprehensive‌ ‌criminal‌ ‌activities‌ ‌

    c) maintain an‌ ‌establishment‌ ‌with‌ ‌the‌ ‌purpose‌ ‌to‌ ‌launder‌ ‌funds‌ ‌collected‌ ‌through‌ ‌comprehensive‌ ‌criminal‌ ‌activities‌ ‌

    A violation of 15(03) is a Felony

    A violation of 15(03) carries a penalty of 12 months of incarceration

    15(04). Wiretapping

    No person shall

    a) conduct surveillance‌ ‌or‌ ‌wiretap ‌in‌ ‌violation‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌one-party‌ ‌notification‌ ‌system‌ ‌without‌ ‌a‌ ‌warrant‌ ‌or‌ ‌proper‌ ‌authorization

    A violation of 15(04) is a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 15(04) carries a penalty of 6 months of incarceration

    15(05). Interference with a Transit System

    No person shall

    a) do‌ ‌any‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌following‌ ‌with‌ ‌respect‌ ‌to‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌property‌,‌ ‌facilities,‌ ‌or‌ ‌vehicles‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌system:

    i) operate,‌ ‌interfere‌ ‌with,‌ ‌enter‌ ‌into,‌ ‌or‌ ‌climb‌ ‌on‌ ‌or‌ ‌in‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌property‌,‌ ‌facilities,‌ ‌or‌ ‌vehicles‌ ‌owned‌ ‌or‌ ‌operated‌ ‌by‌ ‌the‌ ‌transit‌ ‌system‌ ‌without‌ ‌the‌ ‌permission‌ ‌or‌ ‌approval‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌transit‌ ‌system

    ‌ ii) interfere‌ ‌with‌ ‌the‌ ‌operator‌ ‌or‌ ‌operation‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌‌vehicle‌,‌ ‌or‌ ‌impede‌ ‌the‌ ‌safe‌ ‌boarding‌ ‌or‌ ‌alighting‌ ‌of‌ ‌passengers

    ‌ iii) extend‌ ‌any‌ ‌portion‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌body‌ ‌through‌ ‌a‌ ‌window‌ ‌opening‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌‌vehicle‌‌ ‌in‌ ‌a‌ ‌manner‌ ‌that‌ ‌may‌ ‌cause‌ ‌harm‌ ‌or‌ ‌injury., or throw‌ ‌an‌ ‌object‌ ‌from‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌‌vehicle‌

    ‌ iv) commit‌ ‌an‌ ‌act‌ ‌or‌ ‌engage‌ ‌in‌ ‌a‌ ‌behavior‌ ‌that‌ ‌may,‌ ‌with‌ ‌reasonable‌ ‌foreseeability,‌ ‌cause‌ ‌harm‌ ‌or‌ ‌injury‌ ‌to‌ ‌any‌ ‌person‌ ‌or‌ ‌‌property‌

    ‌ v) violate‌ ‌a‌ ‌notice,‌ ‌prohibition,‌ ‌instruction,‌ ‌or‌ ‌direction‌ ‌on‌ ‌a‌ ‌sign‌ ‌that‌ ‌is‌ ‌intended‌ ‌to‌ ‌provide‌ ‌for‌ ‌the‌ ‌safety‌ ‌and‌ ‌security‌ ‌of‌ ‌transit‌ ‌passengers,‌ ‌or‌ ‌the‌ ‌safe‌ ‌and‌ ‌secure‌ ‌operation‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌transit‌ ‌system‌ ‌

    ‌ vi) knowingly‌ ‌give‌ ‌false‌ ‌information‌ ‌to‌ ‌a‌ ‌system‌ ‌employee,‌ ‌or‌ ‌contracted‌ ‌security‌ ‌officer,‌ ‌engaged‌ ‌in‌ ‌the‌ ‌enforcement‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌system‌ ‌ordinance‌ ‌or‌ ‌state‌ ‌law,‌ ‌or‌ ‌otherwise‌ ‌obstruct‌ ‌the‌ ‌issuance‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌citation‌ ‌for‌ ‌the‌ ‌violation‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌system‌ ‌ordinance‌ ‌or‌ ‌state‌ ‌law

    vii) violate‌ ‌any‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌conditions‌ ‌established‌ ‌by‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌system‌ ‌ordinance‌ ‌under‌ ‌which‌ ‌a‌ ‌passenger‌ ‌may‌ ‌board‌ ‌a‌ ‌transit‌ ‌‌vehicle‌‌ ‌with‌ ‌a‌ ‌bicycle‌ ‌and‌ ‌where‌ ‌that‌ ‌bicycle‌ ‌may‌ ‌be‌ ‌stowed‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌transit‌ ‌‌vehicle‌ ‌

    A violation of 15(06) may be charged as an Infraction or a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 15(05) as an Infraction carries a fine of $175

    A violation of 15(05) as a Misdemeanor carries a penalty of a Criminal Summons, or incarceration of 1 month

    15(06). Interference with an Active Runway

    No person shall

    a) impede or obstruct any aircraft while ‌on‌ ‌any‌ ‌airports active ‌runway‌ ‌or‌ ‌heliport‌

    i) the‌ ‌following‌ ‌airports/heliports‌ ‌are‌ ‌‌publicly‌‌ ‌accessible‌ ‌but‌ ‌owned‌ ‌by‌ ‌a‌ ‌government‌ ‌entity‌, ‌and‌ ‌therefore‌ ‌cannot‌ ‌be‌ ‌closed‌ ‌without‌ authorization from the San Andreas Flight Administration ‌following‌ ‌the‌ ‌recommendation‌ ‌from‌ ‌a‌ ‌law‌ ‌enforcement‌ ‌officer

    • Los Santos International Airport
    • Shank Street Heliport
    • Sandy Shores Regional Airfield
    • McKenzie Regional Airfield

    A violation of 15(06) is a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 15(06) carries a penalty of a Criminal Summons, or incarceration of 1 month

    15(07). Tax Evasion

    No person shall

    a) fail‌ ‌to‌ ‌file‌ ‌there‌ ‌Taxes‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌specified‌ ‌date‌ ‌of‌ ‌return‌ ‌without‌ ‌a‌ ‌valid‌ ‌reason‌ ‌ ‌

    b) receive,‌ ‌withhold,‌ ‌destroy,‌ ‌mutilates‌ ‌or‌ ‌falsify‌ ‌any‌ ‌form‌ ‌of‌ ‌documentation‌ ‌that‌ ‌would‌ ‌manipulate‌ ‌and‌ ‌falsify ‌an‌ ‌honest‌ ‌tax‌ ‌return‌ ‌to‌ ‌the‌ ‌state‌ ‌government‌ ‌tax‌ ‌department

    c) purposely‌ ‌destroy‌ ‌or‌ ‌ignore‌ ‌a‌ ‌failure‌ ‌of‌ ‌tax‌ ‌return‌ ‌notification‌ ‌issued‌ ‌by‌ ‌the‌ ‌State‌ ‌Government‌ ‌Tax‌ ‌department‌ ‌

    d) purposely‌ ‌destroy‌ ‌or‌ ‌ignore‌ ‌a‌ ‌failure‌ ‌of‌ ‌tax‌ ‌return‌ ‌notification‌ ‌issued‌ ‌by‌ ‌the‌ ‌State‌ ‌Government‌ ‌Tax‌ ‌department‌

    A violation of 15(07) is a Felony

    A violation of 15(07) carries a penalty of 12 months of incarceration

    15(08). Intentional Damage to State Infrastructure

    No person shall

    a) attempt‌ ‌to or‌ ‌maliciously‌ damage,‌ ‌move,‌ ‌displace,‌ ‌injure,‌ ‌interfere ‌with‌ ‌any‌ ‌State‌ ‌infrastructure

    State Infrastructure is defined as:

    i) Dam‌ ‌walls ‌and‌ ‌accompanying ‌equipment‌ ‌and‌ ‌utilities‌ ‌

    ii) Bridges‌ ‌for‌ ‌Highways,‌ ‌‌Routes‌,‌ ‌Trains,‌ ‌or‌ ‌any‌ ‌form‌ ‌of‌ ‌State‌ ‌Transportation‌ ‌

    iii) Tunnels‌ ‌for‌ ‌Highways,‌ ‌‌Routes‌,‌ ‌Trains,‌ ‌or‌ ‌any‌ ‌form‌ ‌of‌ ‌State‌ ‌Transportation‌ ‌

    iv) Railroad‌ ‌tracks‌ ‌and‌ ‌related‌ ‌locomotives‌

    v) Security‌ ‌barriers‌ ‌into‌ ‌state‌ ‌facilities‌

    vi) Cable‌ ‌and‌ ‌Television‌ ‌lines‌ ‌

    viii) Cable‌ ‌and‌ ‌Telephone‌ ‌lines,‌ ‌including‌ ‌Cell‌ ‌towers‌ ‌and‌ ‌related‌ ‌resources‌ ‌

    viii) Power‌ ‌and‌ ‌Water‌ ‌supply‌ ‌utilities,‌ ‌such‌ ‌as‌ ‌power‌ ‌plants,‌ ‌wind‌ ‌turbines‌ ‌or‌ ‌water‌ ‌pumps‌

    ix) Aviation‌ ‌related‌ ‌lighting,‌ ‌signage‌ ‌and‌ ‌materials‌

    x) Pipes‌ ‌for‌ ‌transportation‌ ‌of‌ ‌gas,‌ ‌water‌ ‌and‌ ‌other‌ ‌utilities.

    A violation of 15(08); subsections iv, v, vi, viii, ix, and x is a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 15(08); subsections i, ii, iii is a Felony

    A violation of 15(08) as a Misdemeanor carries a penalty of 2 months of incarceration

    A violation of 15(08) as a Felony carries a penalty of 12 months of incarceration
