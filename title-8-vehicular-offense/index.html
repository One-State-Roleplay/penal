    A‌ ‌person‌ ‌commits‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌Vehicle‌‌ ‌Registration‌ ‌offense‌ ‌when‌ ‌they,‌ ‌\ **** a) Drive‌‌ ‌any‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌‌ ‌that‌ ‌isn’t‌ ‌registered;‌ ‌or‌ ‌\ i) This ‌includes‌ ‌any‌ ‌‌vehicle‌‌ ‌being‌ ‌used‌ ‌off-road.‌ ‌\ b) Own‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌‌ ‌that‌ ‌isn't‌ ‌registered‌ ‌by‌ ‌the‌ ‌San‌ ‌Andreas‌ ‌Department‌ ‌of‌ ‌Motor‌ ‌Vehicles;‌ ‌or‌ ‌\ c) Sell‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌‌ ‌and‌ ‌does not ‌inform‌ ‌the‌ ‌Department‌ ‌of‌ ‌Motor‌ ‌Vehicles‌ ‌in‌ ‌San‌ ‌Andreas‌ ‌within‌ ‌24‌ ‌hours‌ ‌of‌ ‌selling‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌.‌ ‌\ d) Don't‌ ‌have‌ ‌proof‌ ‌of‌ ‌registration‌ ‌inside‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌‌ ‌while‌ ‌it's‌ ‌being‌ ‌operated‌ ‌inside‌ ‌the‌ ‌State‌ ‌of‌ ‌San‌ ‌Andreas.‌ ‌\

    A‌ ‌person‌ ‌commits‌ ‌Driving‌ ‌while‌ ‌Suspended‌ ‌or‌ ‌Revoked‌ ‌violation‌ ‌when‌ ‌they,‌ ‌\ **** ‌ a) Drives‌‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌motor‌ ‌vehicle‌‌ ‌on‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌highway‌;‌ ‌and‌ ‌\ ‌ b) Has‌ ‌a‌ ‌revoked‌ ‌or‌ ‌suspended‌ ‌license‌ ‌or‌ ‌provisional‌ ‌instruction‌ ‌permit.‌ ‌

    A violation of 8(04) is a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 8(04) carries a penalty of 2 months of incarceration, the vehicle may be impounded

    8(05).‌ ‌Implied‌ ‌Consent‌

    Any person violating 8(05) must be read the Implied Consent Warning before being charged as a violator

    (1) Any individual who accepts the privilege of operating a motor vehicle in the state is deemed to have given his or her consent to submit to an approved chemical test or physical test, including but not limited to

    a) a breath test for the purpose of determining the alcoholic content of his or her blood

    b) a urine test for the purpose of detecting the presence of controlled or prohibited substances

    i) such tests may only be administered by a physician, certified paramedic, registered nurse, licensed practitioner nurse, or other personnel authorized by a hospital to draw blood acting at the request of a law enforcement officer.

    c) an approved blood test for the purpose of determining the alcoholic content of the blood, or for the purpose of detecting the presence of any controlled or prohibited substances

    i) such tests may only be administered by a physician, certified paramedic, registered nurse, licensed practitioner nurse, or other personnel authorized by a hospital to draw blood acting at the request of a law enforcement officer.

    A violation of 8(05) is a Misdemeanor

    A violation of 8(05) carries a penalty of 6 months of incarceration, and a driver's license revocation for 1 year. The vehicle may be impounded

    8(12).‌ ‌Driving‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌Wrong‌ ‌Side‌

    a) Upon‌ ‌all‌ ‌highways,‌ ‌a‌ ‌‌vehicle‌‌ ‌shall‌ ‌be‌ ‌driven‌ ‌upon‌ ‌the‌ ‌right‌ ‌half‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌roadway‌‌ ‌or‌ ‌to‌ ‌the‌ ‌right‌ ‌of‌ ‌the‌ ‌double‌ ‌solid‌ ‌parallel‌ ‌yellow‌ ‌lines.‌ ‌Exceptions‌ ‌to‌ ‌this‌ ‌subsection‌ ‌are‌ ‌as‌ ‌follows:‌ ‌

    i) Yellow markings do not prohibit a driver from crossing the marking if:\ 1) The driver is making a legal left turn at an intersection\ ;or\ 2) The driver is marking a legal U-Turn

    ii) When‌ ‌overtaking‌ ‌and‌ ‌passing‌ ‌another‌ ‌‌vehicle‌‌ ‌proceeding‌ ‌in‌ ‌the‌ ‌same‌ ‌direction‌ ‌when‌ ‌such‌ ‌action‌ ‌can‌ ‌be‌ ‌taken‌ ‌without‌ ‌crossing‌ ‌a‌ ‌double‌ ‌solid‌ ‌yellow‌ ‌or‌ ‌solid‌ ‌white‌ ‌line.‌ ‌

    iii) The‌ ‌‌roadway‌‌ ‌the‌ ‌driver‌ ‌is‌ ‌on‌ ‌is‌ ‌restricted‌ ‌to‌ ‌one-way‌ ‌traffic.‌ ‌

    b) Whenever a road has‌ ‌been‌ ‌divided‌ ‌into‌ ‌two‌ ‌or‌ ‌more‌ ‌‌roadways‌‌ ‌by‌ ‌means‌ ‌of‌ ‌intermittent‌ ‌barriers‌ ‌or‌ ‌by‌ ‌means‌ ‌of‌ ‌a‌ ‌dividing‌ ‌section‌ ‌that‌ ‌measures‌ ‌two‌ ‌or‌ ‌more‌ ‌feet‌ ‌in‌ ‌width‌ ‌and‌ ‌is‌ ‌either‌ ‌unpaved,‌ ‌separated‌ ‌by‌ ‌curbs,‌ ‌double-parallel‌ ‌lines,‌ ‌or‌ ‌other‌ ‌marking‌ ‌on‌ ‌the‌ ‌‌roadway‌,‌ ‌it‌ ‌is‌ ‌unlawful‌ ‌to‌ ‌do‌ ‌the‌ ‌following:‌ ‌
